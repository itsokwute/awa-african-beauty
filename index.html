Your Hair & Style Journey

Welcome to Awa African Beauty & Boutique! I am Hauwa, known to my beloved clients as Awa. With years of dedicated experience mastering the art of traditional and modern African braiding, locs, and twisting styles, my mission is to make you look and feel like royalty.

Beyond salon services, my boutique curation brings a piece of our beautiful heritage to Rio Rancho. Every dress, fabric, and hand-beaded statement necklace in my boutique is handpicked by me for its vibrant design, premium materials, and authentic craftsmanship.

Classic Box Braids

From $160 4-6 Hours

Timeless, clean-sectioned braiding. Highly protective and versatile. Styled to your desired thickness and length.

Knotless Braids

From $180 5-7 Hours

Lighter, scalp-friendly braiding starting flat at the root. Delivers a highly natural look and dynamic flexibility.

Locs Maintenance

From $110 2.5-4 Hours

Cornrows & Feed-ins

From $85 1.5-3 Hours

Sleek, beautiful scalp cornrows or dynamic feed-in styles. Perfect for elegant, quick, and low-maintenance updos.
